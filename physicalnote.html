The survey data are from the Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System (BRFSS), the nation's system of health-related telephone surveys that collect state data about U.S. residents regarding their health-related risk behaviors, chronic health conditions, and use of preventive services. For this indicator, we used the percent of residents who responded “yes” to the question “during the past month, did you participate in any physical activities?” The complete BRFSS data set can be accessed here.
